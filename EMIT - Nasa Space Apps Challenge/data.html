NASA's EMIT Mission: From Raw Telemetry to Earth's Secrets Unveiled - A Data Odyssey

Data Product Description
Level 0 This is the starting point where raw data is collected. Think of it like gathering information from sensors, but it's messy and not organized.
Level 1a Now, we clean up that messy data. We unpack it, remove any extra stuff, and make sure it has a clear timeline. We also add extra information to make sense of it.
Level 1b In this step, we take the cleaned-up data and figure out where it came from on Earth. We also look at how the sensors were pointed when they collected the data.
Level 2a Next, we use the data to figure out what the Earth's surface looks like. We try to remove the effects of clouds and the atmosphere to get a clear picture of what's on the ground.
Level 2b Now, we go even deeper. We analyze the data to figure out what minerals might be on the Earth's surface. We're trying to find out what kind of rocks and minerals are there and ignore anything that's not mineral-related.
Level 3 Here, we take all the information about minerals and create a map. This map shows where different minerals are located on Earth's surface. We also include information about how sure we are of our findings and any potential issues in the data.
Level 4 Lastly, we use all this data to run computer models of Earth's systems. This helps us answer scientific questions and understand our planet better.
